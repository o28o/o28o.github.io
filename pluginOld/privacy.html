Privacy Policy

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The only information it processes is the word that the user clicks on for dictionary lookup. This word is sent to dict.dhamma.gift or dpdict.net and dhamma.gift to fetch relevant dictionary and search results.

  • No other browsing activity is tracked.
  • No personal information is collected, stored, or shared.
  • All requests are made solely to our own managed services.
